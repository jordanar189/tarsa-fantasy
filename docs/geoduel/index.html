GeoDuel

Two-player geography duel for iMessage

Privacy Policy

GeoDuel does not collect, store, transmit, or share any user data.

The app has no accounts, no logins, no analytics, no tracking, no advertising, and no backend servers of its own. Every game runs entirely on your device and is exchanged between players as standard iMessage messages — we have no access to those messages.

Map imagery is provided by Apple Maps Look Around. Apple's privacy policy governs that imagery and any related on-device requests. GeoDuel sends Apple no information beyond what iOS itself uses to render maps and reverse-geocode coordinates.

The optional "GeoDuel Pro" in-app purchase is processed entirely by Apple's App Store. We never see your payment details, billing address, or Apple ID.
